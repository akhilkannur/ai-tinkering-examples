# Video Script: The AI Gym Trainer (The Build Club)

**Target Audience:** Non-technical builders who own AI tool subscriptions but aren't using them.
**Core Message:** Stop being a "donor" to AI companies. Join an execution group that forces you to ship.
**Visual Style:** Tactical Collage (Stop-motion, paper textures, high-contrast text).

---

## Scene 1: The "Donation" (0-5s)
*   **Visual:** Jagged thermal receipts flutter down and pile up on a desk.
*   **Text on Receipts:** 
    *   OpenAI API: -$50
    *   Claude Code: -$20
    *   Moltbot: -$20
*   **Overlay Text:** "CONGRATULATIONS. YOU ARE A DONOR."
*   **Audio:** Sarcastic cash register sounds (*Cha-ching!*).

## Scene 2: The Sleeping Giants (5-10s)
*   **Visual:** Large OpenAI and Claude logos are "sleeping" (pulsing slowly with 'Zzz' bubbles). Small $20 bills fly from the bottom of the screen into the logos.
*   **Overlay Text:** "FEEDING THE BEAST... FOR NOTHING?"
*   **Audio:** Heavy snoring or wind blowing.

## Scene 3: The Coach (10-18s)
*   **Visual:** A Coach's whistle shakes on screen. A clipboard appears. A thick red 'X' slams over the words "WATCH TUTORIAL". A massive red ink stamp slams down.
*   **Stamp Text:** "SHIPPED"
*   **Overlay Text:** "WE FORCE YOU TO SHIP."
*   **Audio:** Loud whistle blow followed by a heavy *THUD* of the stamp.

## Scene 4: The Outro (18-25s)
*   **Visual:** A dark denim/bomber jacket texture. A circular "Build Club" patch (with a hammer icon) slams onto the fabric.
*   **Overlay Text:** "THE BUILD CLUB. EARN YOUR STRIPES."
*   **Call to Action:** "GET TO WORK."
*   **Audio:** Fast, upbeat drum fill or heavy bass beat.
